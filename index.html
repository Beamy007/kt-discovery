Unforgettable Landmarks

Crystal Mosque

A grand structure made of steel, glass and crystal. One of the most iconic mosques in the world, standing majestically on the Wan Man Island.

Terengganu Drawbridge

The first drawbridge in Southeast Asia, connecting Kuala Terengganu and Kuala Nerus. A modern engineering marvel with breathtaking views.

Kampung Cina (Chinatown)

Step back in time through vibrant streets filled with heritage shop-houses, street art, and legendary local delicacies.

The Cultural Soul

Kuala Terengganu is more than just sights. It is the rhythmic sound of traditional boat making, the intricate art of batik printing, and the aroma of authentic Nasi Dagang by the shore. From the vast State Museum to the serene beaches of Pasir Panjang, every corner tells a story of pride and tradition.
